Where to get help with Rivet

  • Mailing list

    The Rivet mailing list is the best place to go for help when you have problems.

    Rivet@tcl.apache.org is the mailing list address.

    To subscribe to the mailing list, send an empty email to Rivet-subscribe@tcl.apache.org.

    Currently, lists are not archived. If you'd like to help out, let us know.
